Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2, 2025

At AxelBase HTML Difference Highlighter, your privacy is not just respected — it is guaranteed by design. This tool operates 100% in your browser. When you paste HTML to compare versions, nothing is uploaded, transmitted, logged, or stored anywhere. This Privacy Policy confirms our complete commitment to data protection.

We Collect Absolutely Nothing

We do not collect, store, or process any form of personal data. This includes:

  • HTML content you paste or compare
  • IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or location data
  • Usage statistics, session information, or analytics
  • Cookies, localStorage persistence, or tracking pixels

All diff calculations happen exclusively on your device using JavaScript. When you close the tab, everything disappears forever.

How the Tool Works

The entire comparison engine — powered by diff2html and jsdiff — runs locally. Even if you disable internet access completely, the tool continues to function perfectly. You can verify this yourself: open Developer Tools → Network tab → no requests are made after page load.

No Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.), advertising networks, error reporting tools, or external fonts that could track you. The only external resources are optional CDN-hosted Bootstrap CSS/JS (which contain no tracking) and the open-source diff libraries bundled locally.

Security & Offline Use

Because no data ever leaves your browser, the tool is safe for:

  • Proprietary templates and internal documentation
  • Pre-release designs and client deliverables
  • Sensitive administrative interfaces
  • Any HTML containing confidential information

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. Since we collect no data, we do not knowingly process information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy only if we add server-side features (which we have no plans to do). Any changes will be clearly dated at the top of this page.

Your data stays yours — always. This is not a marketing claim. It is verifiable architecture.
